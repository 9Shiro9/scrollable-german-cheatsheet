## Key Concepts at A1.1

- **V2 Rule** — The verb always sits in 2nd position in a main clause
- **Cases** — Nominative (subject), Accusative (direct object), Dative (indirect object)
- **Gender** — Every noun is der (m), die (f), or das (n)
- **TeKaMoLo** — Temporal → Kausal → Modal → Lokal (adverb order)
- **Perfekt** — Spoken past tense: `haben/sein` + Partizip II
- **Präteritum** — Written past tense; at A1 only `war` and `hatte`
- **Modal Verbs** — können, müssen, wollen, möchte, dürfen, sollen, mögen
- **Separable Verbs** — Prefix detaches to the end: `Ich stehe auf.`
